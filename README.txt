


# 💙 NHS Supporting Information Generator (v2)

This Streamlit app helps applicants for NHS Jobs generate strong **Supporting Information (SI)** tailored to the job band and person specification.

It uses your **CV** and the **Job Description/Person Specification** to draft a STAR-formatted SI, check your coverage of essential criteria, and ensure you stay within NHS Jobs' character limits.

---

## ✨ Features
- 📂 Upload **Person Specification/Job Description** and **CV** (PDF/DOCX/TXT supported)
- 🧩 **Auto-detects Band** (2–9) or select manually
- ⭐ **STAR auto-formatting** of examples from your CV
- ✅❌ **Essential criteria coverage map** (Strong / Partial / Missing)
- 🔢 **Character counter** (warns if below 1500 or above 3000 characters)
- 💾 Download draft as **.txt** or **.docx**
