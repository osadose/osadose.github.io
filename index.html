About Me

Hello and welcome to my portfolio! My name is Ose Osadolor and I specialize in data science and artificial intelligence. I have 5 years of experience working in the field, and I have a passion for using data and AI to solve complex problems and drive business growth. I have a MSc in Data Science and artificial intelligence with a major on Digital Health from Bournemouth University.     Data modeling and predictions using SQL, SPSS
    Big data processing using Google BigQuery, Spark
    Data wrangling and cleaning
